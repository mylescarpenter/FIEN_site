Hello, we are FIEN, the Foundation for Improving Educational Outcomes for Needy Students

Disability Accomodation

One of our biggest initiatives is disability accomodation. Through fundraisaing and networking efforts we source wheelchairs for schools. Additionally, we contruct wheelchair ramps and sitting toilets. Students in Tanzania are restricted from recieving a proper education, for disabilities that could easily be accomodated with more resources.

Digitization

Digitization of schools is another major initiative of ours. We support schools in sourcing laptops, computers projectors, and hard drives for classroom and computer lab use. One of our staff members, Mr. Salika, is responsible for educating educators on proper protocols to care for and use technology in the school evironment
